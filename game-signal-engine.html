The Vision

Turn Scattered Leads Into Guild Signals.

The Alchemists already notice promising games before they are obvious. Game Signal Engine gives those discoveries a shared path from rough scout note to reviewed game watch page.

Signal Criteria
  • A clear game, game event, launch, update, alpha, tournament, or game community request.
  • Practical reasons members should watch, play, test, create, compete, or form a squad.
  • Enough Discord reaction or watchlist interest to justify publishing a public game page.
Why These Games Matter

Signal Beats Noise

Good games get lost when they live in one message, one DM, or one person’s memory. A shared signal loop helps members compare what is worth watching, who fits the game, and what should move forward.

01

03

For Squads

Four Step Flow

From Scout Note To Published Game

01

Submit

A member adds the game, link, why it is worth watching, and what Alchemists could do around it.

02

Refine

Server-side AI checks that the submission is game-related and shapes it into a concise community-ready draft.

03

Review

Discord reactions and watchlist interest show which games deserve momentum.

04

Publish

Approved signals become `/games/` pages that members can share, watch, and rally around.
