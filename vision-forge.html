How It Works

How Vision Forge Works

01

Share an idea

Start with any idea that could help The Alchemists, support members, create opportunities, improve the community, or bring people together.

02

Refine it with AI

Vision Forge understands who The Alchemists are and helps shape your idea so it fits the community's mission, strengths, and future direction.

03

Post it to Discord

When your idea is ready, Vision Forge turns it into a clean post and sends it to the #vision-forge channel with your name attached.

04

Let the community decide

Members can react, vote, give feedback, offer skills, and help turn the strongest ideas into real community projects.

Your Idea Gets Credit

Every idea posted through Vision Forge gives credit to the member who submitted it. The goal is not to take ideas away from people. The goal is to help the community see them, improve them, vote on them, and collaborate to make them real.

The Community Town Board

The #vision-forge Discord channel is where ideas go after they are shaped. Members can react to vote, reply with feedback, offer their skills, and help decide which visions deserve momentum.

The Alchemists

Vision Forge helps members shape ideas, get credit, and invite the community to build what comes next.
